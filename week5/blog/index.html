On the night he is born, Septimus Heap—the seventh son of the seventh son—is pronounced dead by the midwife and swept away. That same night, the baby's father, Silas Heap, comes across an abandoned child in the snow and brings her home. The girl is ExtraOrdinary Wizard Marcia Overstrand's apprentice and is destined for great things. And as for Septimus Heap… well, that's where it all begins.
